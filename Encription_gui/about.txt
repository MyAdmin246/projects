Introducing "Encryption GUI," a powerful Windows desktop application designed for seamless text encryption and decryption. This user-friendly tool allows users to transform plain text into secure encrypted formats and easily decode them back to their original form. The application supports multiple encryption algorithms, including SHA-1, SHA-2, Morse code, and Atbash cipher, providing versatility for various security needs. Whether you are looking to protect sensitive information or simply explore cryptography, Encryption GUI offers an intuitive interface and robust functionality, making it suitable for both beginners and advanced users. Experience the peace of mind that comes with secure communication and data protection using Encryption GUI.
